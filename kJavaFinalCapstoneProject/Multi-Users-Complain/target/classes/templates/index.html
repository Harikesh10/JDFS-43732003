About CompSolveHub

CompSolveHub is a multi-user complaint tracking and resolution system designed for colleges and organizations.

It enables users to raise complaints, track their progress, and receive timely resolutions, while administrators efficiently manage and resolve issues through a centralized dashboard.

CompSolveHub is built with a focus on usability, role-based access, and communication transparency. By combining intuitive user interfaces with reliable backend processing, the system helps institutions reduce response times, improve issue tracking, and build trust between users and management.

CompSolveHub ensures that every complaint is heard, every issue is tracked, and every resolution is delivered with clarity and accountability.

Centralized Management

All complaints are stored and managed in one place, ensuring transparency and accountability.

Real-Time Email Alerts

Users can track complaint status updates in real time from submission to resolution via mail.

Role-based Access

Separate dashboards for users and administrators ensure secure and efficient complaint handling.
